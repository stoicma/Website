ABOUT ME

Background & Expertise

Venture capital background meets technical blockchain and AI execution.

I'm a consultant specializing in blockchain and AI technologies, with a background in venture capital that gives me a unique perspective on what makes emerging tech companies succeed.

Born in Germany to Bosnian immigrant parents, I've always navigated between worlds—a skill that translates well into bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical business applications. My VC experience taught me how to evaluate opportunities, while my technical work has kept me hands-on with the latest developments in web3 and AI.

I work with companies at various stages: from early-stage startups defining their go-to-market strategy, to established firms looking to integrate blockchain or AI capabilities. My approach combines strategic thinking with technical depth—I don't just advise, I roll up my sleeves and help build.

When I'm not consulting, you'll find me traveling (check out my blog below), staying current with the latest in crypto and AI research, or contributing to open source projects.
