Who Am I?

The big question. I'm an ex-VC guy heavily into blockchain disruption. But that's not really who I am. I'm a 2-hearted, competitive, loving and sometimes lonely person.

I was born in Germany to proud immigrant Yugoslavs. The first German my brother and I learned was in kindergarden. We travelled back to post-war Bosnia twice a year and spent as much time as possible with family. Yet, I'm more "German" than most Germans and I'm proud of it. In Germany, I'm Bosnian. Abroad, I'm German.

I love competing. But my biggest competitor is myself. I don't know how healthy it is to think of your self in 3 tenses, but past-Marko is always an ass. Future-Marko is who I aspire to be, so present-Marko is always busy.

Truly loving someone is one of the scariest things I've ever done. Max potential to be hurt and yet you have to try. I honestly believe the reason we are on earth is to love and share it.

Since I continously try to be a better version of myself, I also spend a lot of time working on myself. Improving some part of my routine, focusing more on nutrition or tracking my sleep. It all takes time and sometimes I forget that there is more to this world.
